Eudia

Transforming the
Legal Industry with AI

Creafting a Product Suite from the zero

When I joined Eudia in April 2025 as Head of Product Design, there was no product yet—just a vision. I built the design foundation from scratch, launching 5 AI-powered legal products in 5 months that established a thriving product business.

Today, we're transforming the legal industry with AI tools known for their intuitive, user-friendly design.

Speed Is the New Moat
Build products in parallel

Drive impact with speed

Products designed with customers, deployed at startup speed. Our first product, M&A, shipped in just 2 months from concept to launch.

Litigation, Sigma, and Compliance followed—each taking just over a month. New integrations like Word plugin, Copilot integration, and Outlook plugin took less than a month each.

Given the competitive landscape, built multiple products at the same time. This enable Eudia to compete with more established players, build a customer base.

AI is reshaping how software is built - both 'how quickly'+'how much' can be built. What took months can be done in hours, needing new ways to design.

Sigma: AI-powered general assistant for lawyers—research, drafting, and day-to-day legal work

Contracting: Draft, review, and manage contracts with AI assistance

Compliance: Automatically flag regulatory risks and align documents with company policies using AI

M&A: Compress weeks of due diligence into days with AI-powered document analysis

Litigation: AI-powered document review, case analysis, and legal research assistance
